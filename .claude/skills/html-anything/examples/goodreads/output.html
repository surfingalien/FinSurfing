Privacy: this page is a self-contained HTML file. Your Goodreads CSV is parsed entirely in your browser by the inline JavaScript on this page. The page does not send your library, your reviews, or any field of your export to any server, ever. Close the tab and the parsed data is gone.

To use it with your own library: visit goodreads.com/review/import, click Export Library, then drop the resulting CSV into the upload control above. The page expects the standard Goodreads export columns (Title, Author, My Rating, Bookshelves, Date Read, Date Added, My Review, Number of Pages, Year Published, Original Publication Year, Average Rating).
